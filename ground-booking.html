Booking Rules & Guidelines

To ensure a seamless experience for all teams and individual players,
please review our standard reserve protocols.

Booking Lead Time

Ground bookings must be reserved at least 48 hours in advance. Net practice sessions can be booked up to 2 hours prior to the slot.

Cancellation Policy

Free cancellations are permitted up to 24 hours before your slot. Late cancellations are subject to a 50% reservation fee.

Required Equipment

Full safety gear (pads, helmets, gloves) is mandatory on all turf pitches. Whites/cricket uniforms are required for stadium match play.

Stadium-Grade Amenities

When you reserve our Main Stadium or Practice Ground, you gain access to first-class supporting systems to host high-quality tournaments and corporate fixtures.

  • Team Pavilion & Dressing Rooms with shower facilities
  • Digital LED Scoreboard and live stream camera mounts
  • On-site first aid facilities and sports physio room
