Building the future of cricket through world-class infrastructure

Our Mission

At CricElite, we believe in democratizing access to professional-grade cricket facilities and coaching. Our ecosystem is built to bridge the gap between amateur passion and professional excellence.

Founded by former international players and sports technology experts, we provide a holistic development pathway that incorporates advanced biomechanical analysis, mental conditioning, and match-scenario training.

Our Journey & Milestones

How we evolved from a localized training academy
into a national benchmark for sports science and elite cricket facilities.

2015

The Genesis

Founded by former cricketers with a single turf wicket and a vision to make professional cricket training accessible to all.

2018

Tech Integration

Introduced slow-motion video analysis, speed sensors, and launched the digital dashboard to track player growth.

2022

Stadium Expansion

Acquired stadium-grade tournament grounds with full night-lighting systems and turf wickets for official match exposure.

2026

National Footprint

Trained over 5,000 athletes, produced 25+ state level players, and partnered with international training clubs.
